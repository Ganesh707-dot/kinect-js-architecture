Execution Context

Creates isolated state for every customer request.

Call Stack

Executes business logic in predictable order.

Async + Promises

Runs multiple services concurrently without blocking.

Event Loop

Allows AI conversations to remain responsive while external services process requests.

Engineering Philosophy

  • Understand customer intent before writing code.
  • Use JavaScript to orchestrate non-blocking AI workflows.
  • Build systems that improve merchant outcomes, not just features.
  • Treat AI as one component of a larger product architecture.

Business value is created by engineering decisions, not by AI models alone.
